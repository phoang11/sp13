Objective
In this portion of Chapter 7, you will grab data from the list containing external HR data and store it in workflow variables to check the number of hours available based on the type of Time Off request.

In this exercise, you will

  • Complete the Time Off Process workflow
Complete the Time Off Process workflow

2.
Click Edit workflow in the Customization section.

Place the orange cursor beneath the Start Workflow stage.
